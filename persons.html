Persons

Names are formatted in the style of published FRUS persons lists: surname first, followed by office, role, and date information where available. Authority entries come from the attached Bush comprehensive names list; missing GCT corpus names are supplemented from current document titles and source-lane evidence.

  • Cheney, Richard Bruce, member, U.S. House of Representatives (R-Wyoming); Secretary of Defense from March 21, 1989
  • Clerides, Glafcos, Greek Cypriot political leader; President of Cyprus from February 1993
  • Demirel, Suleyman, Prime Minister of Turkey
  • Denktash, Rauf, leader of the Turkish Cypriot community
